CORE EXPERTISE

Comprehensive Facade Systems

We offer a highly diversified portfolio of curtain wall systems, customized to meet strict thermal, acoustic, and structural requirements.

Unitized Curtain Walls

Pre-assembled in our factory under strict QA/QC conditions, offering fast on-site installation and superior weather tightness for high-rises.

Stick-Built Facades
